Since 2021

Welcome to Dnyanda English Medium School

Building Confident Learners & Compassionate Individuals

Established in 2021, Dnyanda English Medium School is committed to providing quality English-medium education for children from Grade I to Grade V.

We believe education is much more than classroom learning. Our goal is to develop confident learners, responsible citizens, and compassionate individuals who are ready to face tomorrow's challenges.

Every child is encouraged to explore, question, create, and discover through engaging learning experiences that combine academics with creativity, technology, sports, and life skills.

At Dnyanda English Medium School, every child matters, every dream is valued, and every success is celebrated.
